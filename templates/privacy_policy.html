1. Introduction

Kamate Group Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”, or “Company”) is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us, primarily through contact forms, service requests, or email communication. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address

  • Details of your inquiry or service request (e.g., travel purpose, documentation needs, project requirements)

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., passport numbers, financial details) unless explicitly required for a specific service and provided securely under a separate agreement.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to:

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information only in these limited cases:

5. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. International Data Transfers

Our company is based in Uganda. If you are accessing our services from outside Uganda, your information may be transferred to and processed in Uganda. By using our services, you consent to this transfer and processing in accordance with Ugandan law.
